About Our World in Data:

Our World in Data is an online publication that supplies research and data to make progress against the world’s largest problems. Our work is all public and free; and our visitors range from students and school children doing their classwork, to journalists, teachers, researchers and policymakers, looking for the data and research to inform their work.

Our World in Data is a collaborative effort between researchers at the University of Oxford, who are the scientific editors of the website content; and the non-profit organization Global Change Data Lab, who publishes and maintains the website and the data tools.
